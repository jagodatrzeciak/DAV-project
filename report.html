I. Overview

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of COVID-19 pandemic in Sweden.

The data used in this analysis was sourced from Our World in Data (country-level data) and from the Public Health Agency of Sweden (regional-level data). Some data, such as the number of new cases was collected on a daily bases and some, like the number of vaccinated individuals was collected on a weekly basis. By combining both daily and weekly data, we aimed to create a detailed and accurate picture of the pandemic's trajectory in Sweden.

For most cases, we also compared obtained charts for Sweden with the charts obtained for Poland and other Scandinavian countries like Norway and Finland, which provides a deeper insight.
